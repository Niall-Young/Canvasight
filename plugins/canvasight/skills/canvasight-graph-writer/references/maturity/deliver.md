# Maturity: deliver

Use when the canvas must be executable, handoff-ready, or ready for launch/release work.

## Required coverage

- `maturity.deliver.steps`: ordered work with dependencies and safe parallel branches.
- `maturity.deliver.deliverables`: exact artifacts or external outcomes expected.
- `maturity.deliver.acceptance`: verification evidence and acceptance criteria for each major result.
- `maturity.deliver.risks`: blockers, stop conditions, rollback, and recovery.
- `maturity.deliver.ownership`: owner or responsible role where known; unknown ownership is explicit.
- `maturity.deliver.handoff`: final delivery, documentation, rollout, and unresolved-risk handling.

Do not mark any result complete unless current evidence verifies it.
