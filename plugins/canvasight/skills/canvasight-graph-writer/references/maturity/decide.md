# Maturity: decide

Use when the canvas must support or record a concrete choice.

## Required coverage

- `maturity.decide.question`: exact decision and decision deadline/context.
- `maturity.decide.criteria`: weighted or prioritized evaluation criteria and constraints.
- `maturity.decide.options`: viable options, including status quo when relevant.
- `maturity.decide.tradeoffs`: evidence, benefits, costs, risks, and reversibility for each option.
- `maturity.decide.result`: selected option and reasoning.
- `maturity.decide.rejected`: rejected options and why they lost, or the missing input that prevents a decision.

Do not present preference as evidence or hide unresolved trade-offs.
