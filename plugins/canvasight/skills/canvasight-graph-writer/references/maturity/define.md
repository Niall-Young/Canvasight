# Maturity: define

Use when the result should be sufficiently specified for review or the next discipline to begin work.

## Required coverage

- `maturity.define.definitions`: key terms, actors, responsibilities, and expected outcomes are explicit.
- `maturity.define.rules`: important rules, prerequisites, permissions, limits, and state behavior are defined.
- `maturity.define.flows`: main, alternative, failure, and recovery paths are described where relevant.
- `maturity.define.boundaries`: in-scope, out-of-scope, dependencies, and unresolved choices are explicit.
- `maturity.define.acceptance`: observable criteria distinguish an acceptable result from an incomplete one.

Questions may remain only when named with their impact and next owner/input.
