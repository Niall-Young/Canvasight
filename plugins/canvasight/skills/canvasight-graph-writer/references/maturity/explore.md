# Maturity: explore

Use when the user wants an initial canvas, possibilities, or an editable first outline.

## Required coverage

- `maturity.explore.boundary`: central question, what is being explored, and what is outside this pass.
- `maturity.explore.knownUnknown`: known inputs, assumptions, and consequential unknowns separated.
- `maturity.explore.directions`: distinct candidate directions or organizing hypotheses.
- `maturity.explore.nextQuestions`: prioritized questions that would advance the next iteration.

Exploration may remain open, but it must not be shapeless. Avoid premature execution detail and false decisions.
